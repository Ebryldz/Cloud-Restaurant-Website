ABOUT US

Cloud began its journey in July 2004 with a vision to elevate dining standards and has quickly become a leader in the industry. Through strategic investments, innovative concepts, and strong international partnerships, Cloud Restaurant has established itself as a prominent player both in Turkey and around the world. With a commitment to enhancing the dining experience, Cloud Restaurant brings a fresh perspective to the food and beverage sector. Our diverse range of international collaborations and renowned brands highlights our dedication to offering gastronomic enthusiasts groundbreaking concepts and unparalleled experiences.At Cloud Restaurant, our core philosophy centers around impeccable service, sophisticated design, the highest quality food and beverages, and an innovative approach. We aim to provide each guest with an extraordinary dining experience that sets new standards in the industry.

Today, Cloud Restaurant operates across numerous global locations with a dedicated team of professionals committed to delivering excellence at every level.
